EDEX

Brand re-design

Goal of the project

Redesign the online presence and
creating an educational tool that is
relevant and engaging

Requirments

This project required tons of research on gamification
and ways to implement it into the design process as
well as prove that it works.
Background
The existing EDEX website suffered from usability issues, navigation issues and visual issues. The primary challenge was to redesign the website and encourage users to use the educational tool provided.
Process
My first step was to identify the flaws in the current website where I conducted user testing. Next I researched ways to mediate those flaws and implement them into my design. EDEX did not have any guidelines for what sort of design they wanted so I had to conduct countless tests to see which designs would encourage the user to remain on the website longer as well as working directly with the shareholders to make sure everything is how they imagine it.

OCC

Business Concept

Goal of the project

Creating a fertilizer production and distribution company based in Oman.
The primary focus is on manufacturing high-quality fertilizers to meet the
diverse agricultural needs of the Omani market.

Background

I had the idea to create OCC after I heard that the price of fertilizer has increased
by 4 times. I saw this as a great opportunity to learn more about the world fertilizer
market and understand the needs/wants of its users. I studied and created different
versions of the fertilizer that was being sold such as nitrogen-based, phosphorus-based
and potassium-based fertilizers as a way of understanding what I am trying to market.
I decided to base the company in my home country of Oman as a way of helping to
develop Oman's private market and because I saw it as a way of adding
something to Oman.
Process
I first researched about why the price of fertilizer has
skyrocketed in the past couple of years, followed by
an indepth study on the types of fertilizer and what
would be the most realisitic for a desert. Afterwards I
conducted learned about how to create some
nitrogen-based fertilizer and created my first sample!
Creating it was a difficult process as I had little experience
in chemistry but I was fortunate enough to have a friend
who studied chemistry and he gave me a helping hand when
creating it. I created a business plan and a presentation to present my research and testing to a type of "shark tank" event that was setup in my university and was able to convince the sharks to invest in my idea :)
